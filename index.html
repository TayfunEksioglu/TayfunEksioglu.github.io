THE GOAL OF THE PROJECT

A mobile trading application where people who own agricultural land can offer and sell the remaining agricultural products to the public within a certain period of time and contact customers.

TARGET AUDIENCE OF THE PROJECT

People and the public who own agricultural land.

REASONS FOR THE PROJECT

An agricultural worker who will be harmed by agricultural products can earn income from the remaining product thanks to this application. in this way, the public can have cheaper products

RETURN OF THE PROJECT

• Agricultural products left in the hands of people who own agricultural land do not rot
• People buy organic products cheaper.

HOW THE PROJECT WORKS

• The location information of the seller registered to the system, the product sold, the average price information of the product sold, e-mail, password, official company information, if any, will be able to log into the system.
• The user with the agricultural product has to click the + icon in the profile section and enter at least 1 photo or video, the name of the product to be sold, the kilogram price of the product to be sold, the stock information of the product he sells, and the contact information of the seller, and can publish the advertisement in the application.
• Liked products will come to the fore in the application.
• Name, e-mail, address and password will be requested from the registered recipients.
• After the publication, you can click on the desired ad from the profile information section, where all the ads are located, at any time, and the stock information of the product belonging to that ad, etc. can change.
• Users who need agricultural products can see all the advertisements on the main page of the application, click on the advertisement they want, and view the advertisement information etc. If he wants, he can contact the seller or enter the name of the product he wants on the search page and view only the ads for that product.
